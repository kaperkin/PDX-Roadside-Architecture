Portland Roadside Architecture

Thematic

Thematic Roadside Architecture borrows styles from another time or culture. Thematic styles may be from a single culture or a blending of many. The goal is to create a unique experience through the architecture that marries commercial interests with travelogues. Do you know a building that fits this category? Let us know!

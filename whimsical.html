Portland Roadside Architecture

Whimsical Roadside Architecture uses a common object to form a building or part of a building. The object may represent the business, but not always. This is the most delightful of Roadside works. To be Whimsical, the object does not need to form the entire building, but can simply be incorporated into the design. Do you know a building that fits this category? Let us know!
